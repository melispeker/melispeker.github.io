What is Swift?

Swift is a programming language created by Apple. It was announced in 2014 and has been growing quickly since then.

What is Programming?

Programming consists of inputs and outputs. Inputs are what people provide to the computer. The program tells the computer how to use that information. After processing the inputs, the program produces outputs.

What Are Values?

The inputs and outputs of a program are the information it works with. This is called data. Data is a collection of individual values.

What Are Identifiers?

Naming is an important skill in programming. In programming, you need to create clear, descriptive names called identifiers that reveal the intent of your code.

What are Constants and Variables?

When you create an identifier, you also need to declare whether it's a constant or a variable. The value of a constant doesn't change, while the value of a variable changes.

How Are Simulations Used?

Programmers often use simulations to replicate real-world systems and predict their behavior.

What Are Strings?

In most programming languages, including Swift, text values are called strings and are composed of characters.
