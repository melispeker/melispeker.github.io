Big Idea 1 Explanation Video

① Collaboration
Computing innovations are improved through collaboration.
Effective collaboration produces a computing innovation that reflects the diversity of talents and perspectives of those who designed it.

② Program Function and Purpose
The purpose of computing innovations is to solve problems or to pursue interests through creative expression.
Understanding the purpose provides developers with a better start to develop their project.

③ Program Design and Development
Investigating, designing, prototyping, and testing are commonly used when developing a program.
It is very important to include credit when developing a program.

④ Identifying and Correcting Errors
Logic Error: A mistake in the algorithm or program that causes it to behave incorrectly.
Syntax Error: A mistake in the program where the rules of the programming language are not followed.
Run-time Error: A mistake in the program that occurs during the execution of a program.
Overflow Error: An error that occurs when a computer attempts to handle a number that is outside the defined range of values.
